This work integrates aerodynamic characterization, IMU guidance, monocular visual servoing, and finite-state control for accurate target landing with a low-cost underactuated fixed-wing platform.

{% include figure.liquid loading="eager" path="assets/img/publication_preview/fixed-wing.jpg" title="Low-cost fixed-wing experimental platform" class="img-fluid rounded z-depth-1" %}

The controller was evaluated using CFD-informed simulation, randomized initial conditions, and physical flight tests. The extended manuscript is currently under review at Aerospace Science and Technology; earlier attitude-control results were accepted at CCC 2025.
